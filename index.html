About JKT48

JKT48 is an idol group originating from Indonesia and the first sister group of AKB48 outside of Japan. Adhering to the AKB48 concept of 'idols you can meet,' the group holds regular performances at the JKT48 Theater located on the 4th floor of fX Sudirman, Central Jakarta, DKI Jakarta.
